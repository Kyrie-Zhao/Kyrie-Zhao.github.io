Biography

I am currently a FIRST2nd year Ph.D student in AIoT Lab at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Professor Guoliang Xing. Before, I received my Bachelor degree (2019) in computer science and technology from University of Liverpool and Master degree (2021) from Duke University (Quit Ph.D with Master).

I enjoy exploring and building things that are elegant, novel and impactful, both in research and life. My research is motivated by designing efficient and intelligent systems for core problems in real-world AIoT examples. Specifically, my current focus is on bringing adaptive DNN compiling to multi-DNN inference acceleration on edge GPU.

Research Interests:

  • Edge Intelligence System (EdgeML, ECRT)
  • System for AI (Aaron, BlastNet)
  • AI for System (BalanceFL)
  • DNN Compiler (Moses)
